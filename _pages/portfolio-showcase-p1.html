STAKT

  • Crypto-trading company with limited time of scoping
  • My understanding of cryptocurrency is very minimal
  • Target market were from two different spectrums
  • Balancing as a Product Designer and a Product Owner

Understand

  • Client wanted to cater to users with little to no experience of the industry
  • But also at the same time wanted to cater traders that trade everyday
  • Discovery interviews were done for these two user types
  • Patterns and common pain points were found - users were often overwhelemed and not educated much about the industry, they wanted to feel safe with the idea
  • Understood what competitors were doing well and weren't doing well according to these users - most platforms were extremely overwhelming with no guidance
  • From all the discovery interviews, we were able to identify the most simple and ideal user journey for target market 1 (users with little to no knowledge)
  • We were also able to identify pre-requisites of the application and where major complexity lies for an MVP, which allowed the development team to start their investigations on things that were uncertain whilst I was scoping

Research & Test

  • Competitor research, identify what were the most ideal user experience from discovery interview findigns
  • Brainstorming sessions, identified with the client on all the ideal features and user experience for user type 1
  • Gathering these ideas and turning into wireframes
  • Refine wireframes with client
  • User tested wireframes
  • Re-iterate wireframes
  • Try to nail the user experience and user journey (the main pages at least as development needed to occur within 4 days)

Design and product ownership

  • A limited amount of branding colours and requirements were given
  • A strict design system was identified and created
  • User test the high-fidelity prototype and make minor adjustments and fixes accordingly
  • Balancing and understanding technical requirements and what is feasible
  • Consistently scoping the next requirements whilst working closely with a development team running sprints
