STAKT

  • Crypto-trading company with a unique selling point of targeting users new to the industry
  • Parallel scoping while a sprint runs
  • Role as a Product Designer and a Product Owner

Understand

  • Client wanted to cater to users with little to no experience of the industry as their primary user type
  • Patterns and common pain points were found - users were often overwhelmed and not educated much about the industry, they wanted to feel safe with the idea
  • Understood what competitors were doing well and weren't doing well according to these users

Research & Test

  • Competitor research, identify what were the most ideal user experience from discovery interview findings
  • Gathering these ideas from brainstorming sessions and user journey then turning them into wireframes
  • Refine, test and re-iterate wireframes

Design and product ownership

  • A strict design system was identified and created
  • User test the high-fidelity prototype and make minor adjustments and fixes accordingly
  • Balancing and understanding technical requirements and what is feasible
